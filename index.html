About

Josh Kornish-Sierra is an acclaimed soft-goods designer & product developer based in Missoula, Montana. As a passionate climber & outdoor enthusiast, he designs streamlined gear built for efficiency and longevity in the mountains.

Josh's portfolio includes work for Mystery Ranch, Kletterwerks, Everest Designs, Wills Wings, and Forever Outside; building everything from headwear to military assault packs.
